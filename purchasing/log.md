payments. Drop receipts/invoices in `inbox/`; process them with
`project_management/agent_workflow_scripts/purchasing.md`, which inserts entries
here in date order and updates the spend total below.

`SHIPPING` logistics · `CUSTOMS` clearance · `PAYMENT` financial
